Exploring Los Angeles

August 2024

Back in August 2024, I had the chance to visit Los Angeles—my first time on the West Coast! We started the trip with the magic of Disneyland, where childhood memories came to life again in the middle of August sunshine.

Next stop was Universal Studios, where I geeked out at the Wizarding World of Harry Potter, watched live stunt shows, and enjoyed a behind-the-scenes look at how movies are made.

We also made time to unwind at Santa Monica Beach—a picture-perfect sunset, crashing waves, and a calm that balanced the high-energy theme parks.

One unforgettable moment was watching the LA Kings vs Winnipeg Jets game at Crypto.com Arena. I was the *only one* in my entire section cheering for the Jets! We lost, but I wore my team colors proudly.
